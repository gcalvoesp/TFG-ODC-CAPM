 Item 8.01Other Events.
 
At its regular meeting on June 7, 2023, the Board declared quarterly cash dividends of $0.29 per share of the Company’s Common Stock and $0.218 per share of the Company’s Class B Stock. The dividends will be payable on August 25, 2023, to stockholders of record at the close of business on August 11, 2023. A copy of the Company’s press release announcing these matters is attached as Exhibit 99.1 and the information contained therein is incorporated herein by reference.

The information in this Item 8.01 and Exhibit 99.2 shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the “Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section.
